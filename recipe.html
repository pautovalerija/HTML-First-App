Fetuccini Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 250 g fettuccine
  • salt to taste
  • 50 g butter
  • 100 ml cream - optional
  • 100 g grated parmesan
  • ground black pepper to taste

Cooking

  1. Boil fettuccine in salted water to al dente according to the instructions on the package. Meanwhile, in a saucepan over low heat, melt the butter and then remove from heat.
  2. For creamy sauce, add cream to the butter. Do not remove from heat until pasta is cooked, and stir constantly.
  3. Place the fettuccine in a saucepan using cooking tongs. Turn on medium heat and mix the pasta. Add half the cheese and mix very well again. Sprinkle with the remaining cheese and mix again.
  4. Put the paste on a serving plate and sprinkle with ground pepper.
